Audience Research

The three tasks I want my audience to complete are 1. Posting a New Food on a Map. This is because the primary function of the web application is to advertise the location of leftover food. 2. Use the Resources tab to find other food related resources on campus. Since our project is focused on diversity in accessibility of food, providing other resources serves the purpose. 3. Try to either update or delete a personal food posting. Management of the users postings is crucial to the functionality of the app since users need to provide accurate information on the status and location of the food.

The questions I want to ask the audience members who performed the tests would include 1. How difficult was each of the 3 tasks?; 2. If you had any diffculty performing any of the three tasks what was it. 3. If you were someone looking for leftover food, what information would you like to have in order for you to make a decision; 4. How helpful were the resources on the resources page, do you want more resources?; 5. How difficult was it to edit and manage your posts, what other features would you like to have?
