UC Davis Food Waste Statistics

Every year, the UC Davis Dining Commons (DC) conducts their “Love Food, Don’t Waste” audits. The waste audits take place during the lunch periods of each DC. Every quarter, the DCs collect and analyze data to determine the best ways to reduce food waste.

Fall Quarter’s statistics are usually higher than those in Spring Quarter. After the education throughout the year, there was a visible, overall decrease.

For Spring 2016, the results showed the lowest food waste per person in the history of the waste audits with an average of 1.5 oz per person for all DCs collectively.
