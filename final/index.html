Welcome to UCD FoodMap!

We're very passionate about diversity - specifically diversity in access to food. We believe everyone has the right to have access to healthy food where they live. In a college town like UC Davis, many students may not have the resources to get nutritious food. That's where we come in! We've made a website that shows you where you can get great food for free, in hopes that it will help our fellow students meet their needs. Bon Appetit!

- UCD FoodMap Team
